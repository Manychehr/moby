Sofi: The Future of Finance

Published on October 12, 2021

Sofi is one of the most controversial stocks out there. A hot stock out of its SPAC earlier this summer, things have really cooled for the fintech platform since then. While the stock is up 50% YTD, it has been relatively quiet up until yesterday when it shot up over 13%.

So what happened and are we bullish on the future of this company? Let's dive into our projections and outlook for the young and growing fintech company below.

 

The reason Sofi shot up yesterday was largely to do a strong rating by an analyst in the industry who was bullish on their outlook. Long story short, after much analysis, we fully agree. 

 

Sofi Outlook:

The reason Sofi is so different than their competitors is largely due to one reason: Credit.

Targeting the younger demographic (sub 30 years old), Sofi is tackling an extremely challenging problem. And that is educating younger consumers on the importances of credit and why they should use them vs others. Within this scope, Sofi is specifically looking at credit through the lens of college loans.

This is so challenging because educating younger people on finance in general is hard enough but introducing credit adds in another layer of friction. Their answer towards solving this problem lies mostly in their design as well as their services. Sofi's app is seamless and easy to use and the education & customer service is top notch to say the least. The results so far have been stellar.

Sofi is growing revenues YoY at over 50%! And while this is impressive in itself, one of the more ignored wins of this is the stickiness of their product. While CAC's (customer acquisition costs) are certainly higher than the Robinhood's of the world, churning customers when you lead with credit, is extremely low!

 

Sofi Growth Story & Outlook:

What's more is that once they lock their customers in with credit, they start upselling them the rest of their product suite.

Sofi also offers investing, mortgages and cards and is looking to get a bank charter approved. If they are able to do this, Sofi becomes extremely unique relative to their peers. The difference is that they are your one stop shop for all financial needs whereas Robinhood, Coinbase and others are a one trick pony. While there is certainly value in mastering one niche well, Sofi is choosing to go wide rather than deep and the outcomes are already starting to be extremely strong.

These core competencies lead us to project some serious growth for the platform going forward. The most important metrics are:

  • The first metric is: We project for customer growth to go from 2.6M users today to 5M in the next few years. While we realize almost doubling users in a few years is a lofty goal, Sofi's growth today leads us to believe that this should occur. With the student rent repayment moratorium ending on January 31, 2022, we see a massive amount of users flocking into the platform as soon as this ends! Sofi realizes this and has been preparing for this surge all year.
  • The second metric is: We project over 150% average annual revenue growth in financial services over the next few years. This is going to be lead largely by the spike of users we anticipate over the next few years. Pairing this with some serious tailwinds via their bank charter and the loan moratorium discussed above and we see the setup for Sofi looking perfect! These numbers are a part of our base case thesis and should our bull case play out, we could see this even being 20% higher. With one of the highest growth opportunities over the next few years, we believe the market is undervaluing their upside potential!

 

Sofi Stock Price:

When factoring in all of this together into our sum of the parts analysis (valuing each individual business unit in isolation and then adding them together to figure out what the price should be) we see that Sofi is extremely undervalued relative to where they should be.

Based on the upside we see, we are placing an overweight rating on the stock and believe our valuation is well within reach. While we realize that their valuation is rich for their sector, especially given their business today, we see this growth coming sooner than most expect. Therefore we believe Sofi will grow into their valuation quickly!

Once the two catalysts above start, we see this tumbling into exponentially growth for the company. When they realize Q1 earnings next year, we believe the rest of the market will start catching on and the stock has potential to blow past our price target!

While this does come with significant risk, the upside more than compensates you for your downside exposure!
