Okta: 30% Projected Upside Coming

Published on September 29, 2021

Today we're initiating a new position in Okta. If you're not familiar with Okta, they are the largest identity and access management solution for many of the world's largest companies. They help manage and secure user authentication into company apps. While this technology was always important, remote work made identity management more important than ever. And guess what?

With the stock only up 10% in the last year, the market is completely missing their potential upside.

Let's break into what that upside is and where we project the stock price to go below:

 
 

Okta Market Overview:

Okta's market opportunity and upside is growing as they also grow their business. Based on research from the international data corporation, the identity and access management market should grow to a $44B market by the year 2025. With Okta currently projected to make $1B in revenue by the end of the year and $3B in revenue by 2025 (representing a 30% CAGR), this means that they have only captured 7% of the total market so far. With such a larger opportunity to go after, Okta will have more room to continue to take more market share of a growing vertical. We believe that Okta should be able to do this for two reasons:

  1. The first reason is because we believe that this is the golden moment for Identity and Access Management companies. As these companies shift from nice to have's to central points of security, given the shift from office security to the unsecured remote working confines, companies like Okta will become an irreplaceable part of many companies tech stack. Now that many companies will have to shift from the cheapest option available, to the strongest secure (and often most expensive system), Okta can leverage higher margins will also rapidly expanding revenue.
  2. The second reason is because as more companies shop out their tech stack to outside vendors, they'll need those vendors to integrate with their IAM solution. And guess what - Okta is the market leader of integrations with over 7,000 native integrations across other tech platforms. Therefore their moat starts to become deeper and deeper the larger a company gets integrated with third party providers. This positions Okta as a necessary evil that companies will have no choice but to work with. If they want their identity and access secure, while also working with third parties, there aren't other IAM solutions that have the breadth of integration points that Okta has!

 

Okta Acquisition & Valuation:

While Okta in isolation is extremely defendable for the reasons just discussed, their recent acquisition of Auth0 changes the game for them. This is because Auth0 is the perfect sales funnel for Okta. Having over 40,000 free members, Auth0 sets Okta up perfectly to continue to bring on new members every month. Not only can Okta create new members but they can also cross and upsell the existing ones on their platform! This creates an irreplaceable business development funnel that other IAM solutions do not have. Pair that with a best in class tech and development team and Okta has the ability to completely crush their peers. 

When stitching all of these points together and comparing it against their stock price, we feel that the company is actually undervalued!

Looking at their valuation, we see that the company is trading at 17x forwarding looking sales which comes at a relatively large discount to their competitors. While this pick does come with risk, we see the current valuation as having high reward, thereby fairly compensating you for the risk that you're taking on. Should Okta also deliver on this growth potential, we see them not only increasing their stock price but also expanding their multiple. If this happens, they'll be set up for some rapid growth fast! We're initiating a position now before this "flippening" should occur.
