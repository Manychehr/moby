The Defense Giant Taking Over The Galaxy

Published on July 19, 2021

The company - Lockheed Martin. A household name for some, and unknown for others; they are the military defense contractors who built the F-22A Raptor, the F-35s and the Hercules planes for the US military. However did you know, that the company is much broader than just their fabled aeronautics and missile systems? They've also invested deeply into the space sector - a theme we love here at Moby!

You may be wondering, "What makes them so special amongst the other giants striving to conquer space, and why does it make for an interesting investment case?" Well, we're here to break down that and more below!

Before we discuss the advances in the Space Sector achieved by the company, let us update you with the company’s current operations as well as our latest financial analysis.

 

Overview:

The company operates in four different business segments. They are:

  • Aeronautics: The manufacture of military aircraft still generates the lion’s share of revenue, capturing 40% of 2020 revenue. Popular examples include F-16 Fighting Falcon, F-22 Raptor, F-35 and C-130 Hercules.
  • Missiles & Fire Control: Another segment where the company has stood out – it is credited for the Patriot system, THAAD defense programs, MLRS, etc.
  • Rotary and Mission Systems: This segment manufactures helicopters, surface ships, radar systems, etc.
  • Space: This is the segment we’re most excited about! The revenue share of this segment in the company has been increasing wildly and most recently represented 18% of their revenue. We shall be discussing more about this in the section below!

 

Fundamentals and Performance:

A company with strong financials and good stock performance is always welcome news for us. Let’s look at some remarkable parts of the business:

  • The company managed to improve their revenues from all four segments in Q1 2021! While the addition to an increase in their operating profit was not massively different, we believe that the future growth will continue to expand further. The expected US Military purchases of F-35s, as well as the international demand for the products, will be the reason for the sustainable growth going forward. The European allies, including Switzerland, Poland and possibly Finland, will also be supplied with F35s and/or Patriot defense systems. This will unlock a huge new growing revenue source for them!
  • The stock prices have recently been quite fluctuating. However, after reaching rock bottom in March 2020 courtesy of the pandemic-led crash (it went south of $300), the stock has recovered well. As we still have not seen pandemic level pricing return, we believe the stock should see strong signs of resurgence with an increase in inflation or any increases in the geopolitical climate.

There is little doubt about the stature of Lockheed Martin in the defense sector of the US and many of her allies. However, it is their presence in the space sector that steals the limelight in our post.

 

Lockheed Martin’s Space Adventures:

The company has spearheaded the building of military and commercial satellites – the ones that give early warnings for severe weather, connect troops in a war and also provide GPS services. Lockheed Martin has, however, ventured a few steps further now. Some of their work include:

  • Aeroshells: The company is the proud supplier of the Aeroshells for NASA-led Mars missions. This product keeps the cargo in the spacecraft safe from space’s brunt.
  • Advanced Missile-Warning Satellites: The company recently won a $4.9 billion contract for the construction of these satellites for the US Space Force. The triad of these satellites will be able to make early detection of any ballistic missile launched from anywhere in the world.
  • Autonomous Moon Rover: The much-anticipated NASA Artemis mission, which aims at sending humans to the moon again, will be possibly aided by autonomous moon rovers created by Lockheed Martin. Talk about contributing to science!

The Takeaway:

For a legacy company like Lockheed, it is impressive that they're investing such large swath's of money into growing fields like space. When you combine the vast experience possessed by this company paired with their superior technology and IP that they hold, we surely get a company that has a massive competitive edge over their peers. In such a capital intensive part of the economy, having such hardware costs accounted for gives them a massive moat others will need to swim across just to catch up. We, therefore, feel that Lockheed Martin has a lot to offer as the race to space shifts into another gear! We see this being the spark that ignites Lockheed into the next generation of their company and love them as part of any portfolio.

 

Portfolio Strategy:

While we do not expect Lockheed to be the position in our portfolio that gets us rich overnight, we do expect them to deliver growth and dividends at a LOW RISK profile. That is the key part of this position that we need to call out. While other investments will surely have more upside, Lockheed is an investment that you can add to your portfolio that will lower your risk profile will still chasing upside! In a volatile environment, having a stock like Lockheed will keep your portfolio from blowing up!
